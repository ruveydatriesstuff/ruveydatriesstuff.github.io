Hi! I am a first-year MA student in Linguistics at Boğaziçi University. I completed my undergraduate degree in Linguistics at the same institution in June 2023. I am interested in syntax, semantics, computational linguistics and cognitive science, as well as Japanese linguistics.

Currently, I am working as a researcher at a project led by Dr. Ena Hodzik on predictive processes during sight translation by trainee and professional interpreters, where I mainly help with the collection and cleanup of data using an eye tracker. I am also a part of the weekly Computational Linguistics Reading Group organized by Dr. Ümit Atlamaz where we particulary look at learning models.
